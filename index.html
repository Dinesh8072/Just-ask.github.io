Why websites are important for bussniss?

Websites are vital for businesses as they provide a 24/7 online presence, build credibility, and support marketing efforts. They enhance customer engagement, reflect brand identity, offer a competitive edge, and serve as sales channels. Additionally, websites enable data collection for informed decision-making and strategic growth.

how the static website cost?

The cost of a static website in India varies depending on design complexity, number of pages, and features. Basic static sites usually range from ₹40,000 to ₹1,60,000, while more customized or feature-rich sites can cost between ₹1,60,000 and ₹4,00,000 or higher, based on developer rates and additional requirements.

What is online presence?

Online presence is the visibility of a business or individual on the internet. It includes a website, social media profiles, search engine listings, and digital content. A strong online presence enhances brand recognition, engages audiences, and drives traffic, playing a crucial role in reputation management and business growth.
